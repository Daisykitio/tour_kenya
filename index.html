POPULAR DESTINATIONS

mount Kenya

The second-highest mountain in Africa, Mount Kenya offers thrilling trekking opportunities through lush forests, glaciers, and alpine moorlands. The mountain has three peaks, with Batian and Nelion being technical climbs, while Point Lenana offers a more accessible trek for adventurous hikers. Along the way, you can explore wildlife such as elephants, buffalos, and various bird species.

Maasai Mara National Reserve

The Maasai Mara is one of the most famous safari destinations in Africa, known for the Great Migration, where over 1.5 million wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles traverse the plains between Kenya and Tanzania. The park is home to the Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino) and offers exceptional game viewing year-round. Hot air balloon safaris and visits to Maasai villages add to the experience.

Read more>
Diani Beach

TFor a coastal escape, Diani Beach is a stunning stretch of white sand along the Indian Ocean, located south of Mombasa. It's perfect for relaxing, swimming, or indulging in water sports like snorkeling, scuba diving, and kite surfing. The area also offers luxury resorts and vibrant nightlife, with easy access to marine parks for dolphin watching and coral reef explorations.

Read more>
Amboseli National Park

Amboseli National Park is famous for its iconic views of Mount Kilimanjaro and its large herds of elephants. The park is a photographer's paradise, with a backdrop of Africa’s tallest peak. The observation hill offers panoramic views of the entire park, and visitors can spot giraffes, lions, cheetahs, zebras, and various bird species.

Explore Kenya

Welcome to Explore Kenya, your ultimate guide to discovering the beauty, culture, and adventure that Kenya has to offer. Our passion for travel and deep love for Kenya's rich landscapes, wildlife, and heritage have inspired us to create a platform that connects travelers to the very best experiences in this magnificent country.

Who We Are

We are a team of travel enthusiasts, local experts, and conservationists dedicated to showcasing the hidden gems and iconic destinations that make Kenya one of the most sought-after travel destinations in the world. From the world-renowned Maasai Mara to the pristine beaches of Diani, our goal is to provide you with personalized, authentic experiences that immerse you in the heart of Kenya's culture and natural beauty.

What We Do

At Explore Kenya, we offer tailor-made travel packages, expert advice, and guided tours that cater to every kind of traveler—whether you're an adventure seeker, nature lover, or culture enthusiast. Our services include:

  • Safari Adventures: Experience the thrill of Kenya's famous wildlife in national parks like the Maasai Mara, Amboseli, Tsavo, and more.
  • Cultural Tours: Engage with Kenya's diverse communities, learn about their traditions, and explore the rich cultural heritage that makes Kenya unique.
  • Beach Holidays: Relax on Kenya's stunning coastline, from the tranquil shores of Lamu to the vibrant Diani Beach.
  • Mountain Treks: Conquer Mount Kenya and other scenic treks that offer breathtaking views and encounters with wildlife.
  • City Excursions: Discover the bustling energy of Nairobi and Mombasa with city tours, historical site visits, and modern urban adventures.

Why Choose Us?

Local Expertise: Our team consists of local guides and experts who know Kenya inside out, ensuring that you get the most authentic and insightful experiences.
